When a creature you can see within 60 feet makes an attack roll, ability check, or damage roll, you can use your reaction to expend one Bardic Inspiration use, rolling a Bardic Inspiration die and subtracting the number rolled from the creature's roll. You can choose to use this after the creature rolls but before the DM determines the result or damage is dealt. The creature is immune if it cannot hear you or is immune to being charmed.
