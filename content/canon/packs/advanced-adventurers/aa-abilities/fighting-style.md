You adopt a particular style of fighting as your specialty. Choose one of the following options. You can take this ability multiple times but must choose a different style each time.

- **Archery** - +2 bonus to attack rolls with ranged weapons.
- **Defense** - +1 bonus to AC while wearing armor.
- **Dueling** - +2 bonus to damage rolls when wielding a melee weapon in one hand and no other weapons.
- **Great Weapon Fighting** - Reroll 1s and 2s on damage dice for two-handed or versatile melee weapons (must use new roll).
- **Protection** - Use reaction to impose disadvantage on an attack against a target within 5 feet (requires shield).
- **Two-Weapon Fighting** - Add ability modifier to the damage of the second attack when two-weapon fighting.
