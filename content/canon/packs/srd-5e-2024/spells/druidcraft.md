Whispering to the spirits of nature, you create one of the following effects within range. _**Weather Sensor.**_ You create a Tiny, harmless sensory effect that predicts what the weather will be at your location for the next 24 hours. The effect might manifest as a golden orb for clear skies, a cloud for rain, falling snowflakes for snow, and so on. This effect persists for 1 round. _**Bloom.**_ You instantly make a flower blossom, a seed pod open, or a leaf bud bloom. _**Sensory Effect.**_ You create a harmless sensory effect, such as falling leaves, spectral dancing fairies, a gentle breeze, the sound of an animal, or the faint odor of skunk. The effect must fit in a 5-foot Cube. _**Fire Play.**_ You light or snuff out a candle, a torch, or a campfire.
