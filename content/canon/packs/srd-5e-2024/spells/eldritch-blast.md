You hurl a beam of crackling energy. Make a ranged spell attack against one creature or object in range. On a hit, the target takes 1d10 Force damage.

At higher levels, the spell creates additional beams. You can direct the beams at the same target or at different ones. Make a separate attack roll for each beam.
