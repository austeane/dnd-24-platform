In your study of occult lore, you have unearthed eldritch invocations, fragments of forbidden knowledge that imbue you with an abiding magical ability.

When you choose this ability for the first time it costs 17 exp and you choose two invocations from the list. You may acquire this ability additional times; it costs 7 additional experience each time after the first and you gain one more invocation.

Individual invocations (such as Agonizing Blast, Repelling Blast, Devil's Sight) are tracked as separate AA abilities linked to this purchase path.
